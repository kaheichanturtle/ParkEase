The Magic Behind the Timer

ParkEase uses a clever, privacy-focused technique to ensure your timer works reliably in the background, based on Mediatick

1

Silent Audio Loop

By playing a silent audio track, ParkEase keeps a media session active, allowing it to run processes in the background without draining your battery.

2

Seamless Alarm

When your parking time is nearly up, the app seamlessly swaps the silent track for an audible alarm, ensuring you get notified even if your phone is locked.

3

On-Device Processing

This entire process happens on your device. No data is sent to a server, guaranteeing your privacy and making the app incredibly fast and reliable.
