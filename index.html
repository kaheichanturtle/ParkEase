How It Works

Your personal parking assistant—powered by AI, maps, and smart automation.

1

Snap a Photo

Capture a clear photo of any parking sign using your phone’s camera. Our app auto-detects the sign area instantly.

2

AI Analyzes

Using advanced AI (powered by Google's DeepMind), we decode the rules in real time, adjusting for day, time, and location.

3

Track & Remind

Your parking session runs in the background with GPS pin drop, a timer, and a pre-expiry alert. Everything’s logged in your parking history.

The Magic Behind Background Operation

Thanks to the innovative BackgroundSiter implementation from kaheichanturtle's BackgroundSiter (github.com/kaheichanturtle/backgroundsiter), we use a looping silent audio file to keep the media bar active. When your parking time is up, it seamlessly transitions to an alarm sound.
